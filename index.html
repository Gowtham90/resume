About Me

It gives me great pleasure to introduce myself as Geddadi Gowtham Kumar, born on 1st January 1989. I hail from Visakhapatnam, India.

I have done my studies till 12th class in Visakhapatnam, India and then chose to follow my brothers in pursuing engineering. With my hard work and a little luck i made it to one of the top five universities in India. I graduated from the National Institute of Technology Warangal India in the stream of Metallurgical and Materials Engineering with First class marks.

I was very much interested and passionate about making my career in software industry since schooling. The targets given were that of meeting commissioning dates and balancing the quality of project and execution of works, alongside safety measures. This experience has endowed me with rich practical experience of possible disruptions, planning/execution of project and developing of my interpersonal & team leader qualities.
